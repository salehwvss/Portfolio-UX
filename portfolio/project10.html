BlueRoute

BlueRoute is your real-time urban travel companion. Navigate your city’s transport system with accurate real-time predictions, simple trip planning, step-by-step navigation, service disruption notifications, and departure and stop reminders... all presented in a clear, bold interface.
We strive to provide a full featured app to tackle our customers’ problems
